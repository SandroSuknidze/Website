FACULTY OF BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGIES

The faculty strives to provide the opportunities to gain education, which meets worldwide recognized, modern standards; to train competitive and highly- qualified specialists at Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctoral programs; to create student-centered academic environment, provide academic freedom and ensure student competitiveness on international employment market.
Students are offered programs at three educational stages:
  • Undergraduate Programs
  • Master's Programs
  • PHD Programs
